1. Shipping Partners

We partner with reliable Philippine couriers to ensure safe and timely delivery of our premium cashew products:

  • J&T Express - Fast and reliable nationwide delivery
  • Lalamove - Quick same-day delivery for Metro Manila

All our shipping partners are trusted names in the Philippine logistics industry, ensuring your cashews arrive fresh and intact.

2. Delivery Areas

Local Delivery (Metro Manila):

  • Fast delivery within 1-3 business days
  • Same-day delivery available in select areas

Provincial Delivery (Philippines):

  • Nationwide coverage across all Philippine provinces
  • Delivery timeframe: 3-7 business days, depending on location
  • Remote areas may require additional 1-2 days

3. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on several factors:

  • Weight: Total weight of your cashew order
  • Distance: Delivery location from our warehouse
  • Order Size: Quantity of products ordered

Free Shipping Promo: Free shipping may apply for bulk orders (e.g., 5kg or more of cashews) through our retail partners. Ask about current promotions!

Additional Fees:

  • Insurance for high-value orders
  • Express delivery surcharge
  • Remote area delivery fees (if applicable)

4. Packaging and Handling

We take great care in packaging our premium cashews to ensure they arrive in perfect condition:

  • Eco-Friendly Boxes: Secure, sustainable packaging materials
  • Freshness Protection: Cashews shipped with cooling packs to maintain freshness during transit
  • Damage Prevention: Multiple layers of protection to prevent crushing or damage
  • Tracking Numbers: Provided for all shipments so you can monitor your order

Important: Cashews are perishable items. All orders are carefully handled with temperature control to preserve quality and taste.

5. Estimated Delivery Times

Processing Time:

  • Orders placed before 2 PM are processed the same day
  • Orders placed after 2 PM are processed the next business day

Delivery Schedule:

  • Monday to Saturday: Regular delivery schedule
  • Sundays and holidays: No deliveries

Possible Delays: Delivery times may be affected by weather conditions, holidays, peak seasons, or high demand periods. We'll notify you immediately if any delays occur.

6. Tracking and Support

Track Your Order:

  • Use your tracking number to monitor your package on the courier's website
  • Receive real-time updates on your delivery status
  • Get notifications when your order is out for delivery

Need Help?

  • For shipping issues, contact your retailer first
  • For general inquiries, reach out to us directly
  • We're here to ensure you receive your cashews in perfect condition
