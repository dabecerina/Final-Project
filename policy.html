Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 2025

Welcome to Cashew Manila! This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or contact us. We are committed to keeping your data safe and transparent. Our site is designed for everyone and is user-friendly, focusing on advertising our cashew products and providing easy ways to reach us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to keep things simple:

Contact Information: If you message us via Facebook, Instagram, or call our phone number, we may receive your name, message content, or contact details you provide.

Website Usage: We may use basic analytics tools (like Google Analytics) to track general site traffic, such as pages visited or device type, but we don't collect personal identifiers unless you share them.

No Forms or Accounts: Our site doesn't require sign-ups, so we don't store emails, passwords, or payment info.

We don't collect sensitive data like financial details or location tracking without your consent.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect only for:

  • Responding to your messages or inquiries about our cashew products.
  • Improving our website and advertisements to make it more user-friendly.
  • Ensuring the site works well for everyone (e.g., fixing bugs or optimizing for mobile).

We don't sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Sharing Your Information

We keep your information private and only share it when necessary:

  • With service providers (e.g., our web host or social media platforms) to operate the site.
  • If required by law (e.g., to comply with Philippine data privacy regulations).

We won't share your data with advertisers or other businesses.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, such as using secure hosting and not storing unnecessary data. However, no online method is 100% secure, so contact us if you have concerns.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access or request deletion of any personal data we hold.
  • Opt out of any future communications.
  • Contact us to update or correct information.

To exercise these rights, reach out via our Facebook page, Instagram, or phone number.

6. Children's Privacy

Our site is for everyone, but we don't knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we learn of such data, we'll delete it promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We'll post changes here and notify you via our social media if significant.
