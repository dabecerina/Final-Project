Return and Refund Policy

Last Updated Date: August, 2025

At Cashew Manila, we prioritize food safety and customer satisfaction. As a food website specializing in perishable items like cashews, returns and refunds must be submitted immediately (within 48 hours of delivery) to report any issues, such as defects, damage, or dissatisfaction. This ensures we can address concerns promptly before spoilage or health risks arise. This policy complies with Philippine consumer protection laws, including your right to return defective or misrepresented products within 7 days of delivery.

1. Eligibility for Returns

Return Window: You may return items within 7 days of receiving your order for a full refund or exchange.

Immediate Submission Requirement: For all food-related returns or refunds, you must contact us within 48 hours of delivery with details and photos of the issue. Delays may void eligibility due to spoilage risks.

Eligible Items:

  • Unopened, unused, and in original packaging.
  • Not applicable to perishable items like cashews if they have been opened, stored improperly, or show signs of spoilage.

Non-Eligible Items: Custom orders, personalized products, or items damaged due to misuse.

Proof of Purchase: A valid order number and receipt are required.

2. How to Initiate a Return

  1. Contact Us Immediately: Email us at returns@maycashewmanila.com or call [your phone number] within 48 hours of delivery. Provide your order details, reason for return, and photos (e.g., of damaged packaging or product).
  2. Approval: We'll review your request promptly and send a return authorization (RA) number via email if eligible.
  3. Packaging: Pack the item securely in its original box. Include the RA number and a brief note explaining the issue.
  4. Shipping: Ship to our address: 123 Cashew Street, Manila, Philippines. You are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or our error. We recommend using a trackable service like LBC or J&T Express.
  5. Processing Time: Returns are processed within 5-7 business days after receipt.

3. Refunds

Refund Amount: Full refund for eligible returns, including the original purchase price minus any shipping fees (unless we covered them).

Processing: Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 7-10 business days after approval. Bank transfers may take 3-5 additional days.

Currency and Fees: Refunds are in Philippine Pesos (PHP). Any currency conversion fees are not reimbursed.

Store Credit Option: As an alternative, you can opt for store credit valid for 6 months.

4. Exchanges

Eligibility: Same as returns. We'll exchange for the same item, a different size/flavor, or a comparable product.

Process: Follow the return steps above. Exchanges are shipped at no extra cost once approved.

5. Damaged or Defective Items

Immediate Action: Report issues within 48 hours of delivery with photos.

Resolution: We'll replace the item or refund promptly, covering return shipping.

6. Exceptions and Limitations

Perishable Goods: Cashews are perishable; returns are not accepted if spoilage is due to improper storage or delayed reporting. Immediate submission is mandatory to protect food safety.

International Orders: Additional customs fees or duties are non-refundable.

Abuse of Policy: Excessive returns may result in account restrictions.

Disputes: Unresolved issues can be escalated to the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI) Consumer Protection Division.
