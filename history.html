OUR STORY

Decades of Passion, Tradition & Excellence

The Cashew

Cashew was established because cashews are among the rarest, most unique, and hardest-to-find items in Manila. The business began in 2008, initiated by the founder's grandmother, with initial products consisting of onions and goods from a sari-sari store. It was the founder's mother who innovated and sought out new products, noticing a high demand for cashews among buyers. She decided to become a supplier, sourcing them and eventually expanding the offerings to include various nuts.

The original location was in the Lumang palengke, situated in I Mall during the era of the old palengke. However, due to Leyble, the store closed in 2015, and the palengke relocated across from Robinson to the new palengke. The family then searched for ways to resume sales, eventually securing a spot within the old palengke and town area, where operations continued. Today, the store is conveniently located in Sta. Ana Bldg., F. Manalo St., Brgy. San Jose, Antipolo City, making it easily accessible for customers in the region.

Later, the mother fell ill but continued managing the business, while her son began selling products online to support the family's finances. After the mother's passing, the family honored her legacy by continuing to run the store with the same passion and dedication she had shown throughout the years.

Today, Cashew Manila stands as a testament to three generations of hard work, resilience, and love. From a grandmother's sari-sari store to a mother's vision for premium nuts, and now carried forward by the next generation, our story is one of family, perseverance, and an enduring commitment to bringing the finest cashews and nuts to Filipino homes. We've earned national recognition, expanded internationally, and built a legacy that continues to grow but our commitment remains unchanged: Made with Hearth, Taste Like Gold. Every product we sell carries the spirit of those who came before us, and their legacy continues in every carefully selected nut we offer.
