Business Overview

Welcome to Manila Cashew Business! Founded in 2010 in Manila, Philippines, we are a family-owned business specializing in premium cashew products sold exclusively within the Philippines. We source high-quality cashews from both local Filipino farms and trusted international suppliers to create a diverse range of snacks, nuts, and processed goods. Starting small with a focus on community-driven agriculture, we've become a beloved local brand, delivering fresh, flavorful cashews to Filipino households, retailers, and food businesses nationwide.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: Cashew Manila is committed to offering high-quality, locally sourced cashews and other nuts through retail and wholesale. We aim to build lasting partnerships with retailers and wholesalers by ensuring freshness, affordability, and excellent service in every transaction.

Vision: To be the leading nut supplier in the Philippines, known for providing the finest quality cashews and nuts at affordable prices that delight every customer and partner.

Achievements & What Sets Us Apart

Since 2010, we've built a loyal customer base across the Philippines, earning recognition for our blend of imported and local cashews that offer unique flavors and nutritional benefits. What sets us apart? Our exclusive focus on the Philippine market allows us to tailor products to local tastes, with zero-waste processing and direct support for Filipino communities. We've partnered with local schools for nutrition programs and contributed to agricultural development in the region.
